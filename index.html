What We Do

Research Interests

We combine cognitive science, psychology, robotics, and AI to explore emotion-aware intelligent systems — from theory to real-world applications.

Affective Computing

Multimodal emotion recognition from speech, vision, and text using deep graph neural networks and transformer architectures.

Social Robotics & HRI

Designing emotionally expressive humanoid robots that modulate authority, warmth, and social connectedness through bodily movement.

Mental Health AI

Automatic depression detection systems leveraging facial action units, physiology signals, and personality-aware multimodal frameworks.

Dialogue & Conversation

Dialogue act classification, emotional voice conversion, and rapport-building conversation systems for human-agent interaction.

Agent Expressive Expression Generation

Emotional voice generation, expressive gesture synthesis, and emotional talking head for interactive agents.

Cognitive Science

Incorporating psychological and cognitive models to optimize interaction strategies between humans and intelligent agents.
